About NutriData

NutriData was inspired by our love for healthy living, cooking and exploring new cuisines. We wanted to create a platform that makes it easy for food enthusiasts to discover, share recipes and maintain a healthy lifestyle. This project was developed as a portfolio project for Holberton School, where we honed our software engineering skills.
